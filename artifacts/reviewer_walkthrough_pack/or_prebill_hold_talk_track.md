# Reviewer Talk Track: OR Prebill Hold Story

Claim: this product can carry one end-to-end control-room story from summary, to opened proof, to memo language without changing what happened or who owns it. Proof: the featured story on `Control Room Summary` picks `QUEUE-ACC-1025` in outpatient surgery, shows a billing/claim-edit failure caused by workflow handoff, and says the account is stuck in `Modifiers / Edits / Prebill Holds` under `Billing operations`, `7` days overdue, and already `Post-window financially lost`. When you open the representative proof, the case narrative and classification repeat the same reason it surfaced: documented performed activity supports the expected primary facility procedure charge, but the actual charge is still held prebill and the current blocker is unresolved. The `Revenue Integrity Decision Pack` then preserves that same issue domain, root cause, blocker, owner, aging, recoverability, and next action in the `Current control story` section. Caveat: this is one synthetic/public-safe frozen V1 story, so it proves deterministic coherence and traceability, not live source integration or broad production coverage.
